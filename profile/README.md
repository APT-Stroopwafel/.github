# APT Stroopwafel *0

**APT Stroopwafel** is the first Dutch advanced persistent threat organization created to represent the Netherlands in cybersecurity research, red teaming, and threat simulation.

## About

We are a specialized group focused on high-fidelity offensive security operations, vulnerability discovery, and threat emulation. Our work is inspired by Dutch ingenuity, collaboration, and the shared goal of making digital infrastructure more resilient.

## Mission

- Demonstrate world-class offensive security capability from the Netherlands.
- Advance cybersecurity through realistic red team exercises and community knowledge sharing.
- Promote responsible disclosure, defensive readiness, and stronger protection across public and private sectors.

## What We Do

- Develop and test tailored adversary emulation techniques.
- Research vulnerabilities in software and systems.
- Share insights with defenders through transparent reporting and community engagement.
- Foster a stronger local cybersecurity community with Dutch perspective and expertise.

## Why Dutch?

The Netherlands has a rich history of innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence. APT Stroopwafel stands proudly as the first Dutch APT-style initiative, combining local talent with global threat intelligence to raise the bar for cyber defense.
